Overview

This is an overview of frags, deaths, suicides and events that happened to this player. Also shown are the nettos (= f - d - s), incidents (= f + d + s), and netto% (= (f - d - s) / (f + d + s)) values. See Help for details.

Weapon Usage

Here you will find details on this players weapon usage frags, how hard he was hit by the other player's weapons deaths, and the mistakes he made suicides. See Help for details.


Suicides

Here you find the suicides split into the for this game/mod possible damage types. Note: Only the suicides that actually happened to this player are shown. See Help for details.

Events

A recent addition to æstats are events. These non-frags track many of the other things that happened on this server, also used for team stats. See Help for details.

Roles (player classes)

Another recent addition to æstats are roles. for each happening the player classes are tracked, to better show this players gaming habits. See Help for details.

Role correlation (frags/deaths)

This matrix shows the player roles (classes). Role fragger's are on the x-axis (lines), and the role fragged are shown on the y-axis (columns). This will let you interpret what the most commonly used frag roles were. If both players were of the same class, then they are shown on the diagonal. See Help for details.

Streaks (f/d/s in a row)

Ever wondered how many frags in a row you where fated to hand out, or how often you died/suicides in a map? Check your streaks below. See Help for details.

Alternate weapon usage

Some games/mods have weapons with an alternate means of attack. if possible æstats will show the these alternate weapon related frags, deaths, and suicides. See Help for details.


FPH (Frags Per Hour) per map

Here the last maps this player played are shown (upto a maximum of set in AEstats.cfg). The histogram shows the FPH (Frags Per Hour, using the netto (= f - d - s) count) per map. The other table floods you with details on the last few maps. Like your top frag/death streaks (F-Row, D-Row). See Help for details.
